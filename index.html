Deadlock is awesome
Valve's 8th project, Deadlock, is an awesome game that combines the best elements of MOBA into a fast, team-based shooter.
Some random information
AbramsAn absolute early-game beast who dominates at close-range using his lifesteal, melee attacks and stuns.
IvyA long-range support hero who provides area denial, buffs and healing for her teammates.
KelvinA high mobility support hero who helps his team by slowing the enemy and stopping their advance.
BebopA roaming carry who disrupts the enemy by hooking them out of position and bursting them down with his close-range abilities.
