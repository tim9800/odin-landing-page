Deadlock is awesome
Valve's 8th project, Deadlock, is an awesome game that combines the best elements of MOBA into a fast, team-based shooter.
Some random information.
Abrams. An absolute early-game beast who dominates at close-range using his lifesteal, melee attacks and stuns.
Ivy. A long-range support character who provides area denial, buffs and healing for her teammates.
Kelvin. A high mobility support character who helps his team by slowing the enemy and stopping their advance.
Bebop. A roaming carry who disrupts the enemy by hooking them out of position and bursting them down with his close-range abilities.
Deadlock is such a great game. I've spent all of my last three weekends playing this game with my friends. Now my sleep schedule is all out of wack. But the pain is worth it. Trust me.
-Anonymous Deadlock Enjoyer
